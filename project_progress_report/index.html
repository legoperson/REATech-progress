How to Read the Headline Numbers

These four headline metrics describe the most updated retained model in the current project. It is the relaxed base-pool correlated-suburb model with overlap=2, building-bedroom level prediction, unit adjustment, and post-estimation validation. In this context, overlap=2 means at least two historical observations overlap between the target building-bedroom series and a candidate suburb base series.

Raw coverage means the share of test rows that receive a prediction before uncertainty filtering. Retained coverage means the share of all test rows that remain after the validator removes uncertain predictions. Post-filter MAPE is the error on that retained, higher-confidence subset.
